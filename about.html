About Us

Kendriya Vidyalaya No.2, Itanagar, Arunachal Pradesh was established in the year 1983-84 and is situated at Chimpu village 4.5 Kms far from Ganga Market (A main market in the Itanagar City) in a lap of beautiful mountain ranges. The School is run by the well-qualified teachers in all the subjects along with Work Education (Vocational Education), Arts and drawing, Physical Education and Training, Music etc. in full range of norms of KVS. The Vidyalaya is having magnificent infrastructure facilities including maintained building with well furnished class rooms, well equipped laboratories, well developed play ground. Streams available: Class-I to Class-X of 3 sections each and Class-XI and Class-XII of two sections each(Science Stream Only).
